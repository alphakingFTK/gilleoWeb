Uploading Guidelines

These guidelines to uploading videos help map out contents better for Users and Creators. The title and genre of the videos help viewers get their targeted type of videos with less noise from videos that would not fall under those catalog.

Creators must fill each video properly with no false cataloging or tags added. Any that is found will be removed from view and ask to be fixed.

Some of these features will be present on release, others will be implemented over time. Videos uploaded after the new features are implemented must fill them out as requested, videos before are not but may show up less if not updated.

Average video will need to be 10 minutes long max. The video will require 1k views before Creators can monetize if they qualify.

Understand that this is an open platform and you are responsible for everything you upload to it. We will treat any violation with the utmost of the law to protect ourselves and other user's of this platform. We want everyone to use as much common sense this world still has to determine if it's okay to upload, or just look up what is Ok/Blacklisted on our platform. It's available to everyone and it's free.

VIDEO INFO

Channel: Creators will be able to make up to 4-5 channels. During upload, creators will choose which channel the video will upload too. Channels are seen as individual accounts and are treated as so. We will promote each Channel over the top "Spotlight", and continue to unless THAT Channel breaks the terms "or" the user profile in whole have been proven to be of violation.

Series: The Creator will check this box if this video is part of a series, which when chosen will show more opinion to choose the session and episode. The series will be given a title and will be there for future selections when more episodes are loaded to it.

  • ● Series Title: This will be where the name of the Show name and the name the playlist it creates goes by.
  • ● Session: Unlocked when Series is chosen. User will give a session number.
  • ● Episode: Unlocked when Session is chosen. It will be given an episode number to go along with the series.(1-24)

    • Titles: the video name must clearly point out what is the main point of the video

    Description: This will be used to give details and highlights of the video and it's content.

    Audio License: This section is to input purchased license code for presentation to any company that may try to strike your videos for music used in it that you gain the rights to use. No more than 3 License (music) per video.

    Production Team: Here you give credit to staff and other Creators. If they are on the platform you can use their ID# to link them directly to their profile

    Genre: Video genre to properly catalog them, putting in the correct ones will help it be found faster by viewers of that type of fan based

    VIDEO RATING

    Rating: The level of Parental Control for age appropriate to age restriction of the content of the video.

  • ● Mature: has graphics, vulgar language content that some may find to be excessive or offensive. Dark humor based, Best suited for people 18+. Kinky, erotic base content also falls under this rating as so do heavy expression of sexual means or language. Stuff that seems extremely violent will be tagged under this rating.
  • ● Teens: has some to a moderate cursing and some adult base content not safe for kids under 13yrs old. May not have any open adult influence talking of sex or education of such
  • ● Youths/Family: has no cursing and is targeted for kids less than age 13yrs, plus good for family gathering

    • When a user views a video with a Gilleo content rating, the page displays the Rating for each video that has a value other than None. This can be partnered up with detailed rating letters to point out key content that may be in the video that the brode ratings don't specify.

    The video letter rating categories are listed below:

  • ● Nudity (N) -5%
  • ● Graphic Violence (GV) -15%
  • ● Graphic Language (GL) -1.5%
  • ● Strong Sexual Content (SC) -8.5%

    • For categories where you've chosen the third option, a plus sign appears after the Rating. For example, the Gilleo content rating for a video with explicit language would appear as "Mature", but with nudity and such would appear as "Mature+" when click a info bubble will pop up to give more details with the letter telling what is in this rating as extra content. Even though the rating tells what should be expected, some videos may have excessive content that needs to be emphasized. This will allow the Creator to inform the viewers ahead of time.

    * Graphic videos must be censored to some level and somewhat if it includes a person being injured. Exceptions are as follows, legal hunting of wild game or farming traditions, common sense must be a factor of choosing the level of the graphic viewing. We will not permit anything out of the legal standards.

    * The percentage is to indicate the subtract percentage of visibility based on the type of content from 100% visibility rate
