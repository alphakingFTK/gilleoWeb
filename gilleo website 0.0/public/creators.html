Creator Zone (C.Z.)

Welcome Creators, this is a quick run down of futuer plans for the Gilleo "Partner Program" and other information users may like to know of.

Gilleo will work to provide its best platform possible by an independent company. We will try to hold all rules listed in “Terms Of Use” to the fullest for every user and Creators that we are here to serve. All videos must be uploaded within the guidelines provided in “Upload Rules”. As such no users will be treated and different by status or partnership if any violations are present.

To keep costs low and keeping the best possible experience on this platform, each year we will have a digital flush. That means any channels/videos with less than 500 following (or 15k views) will be removed from the platform from the previous year. Videos that are more than two (2) years (that have not received Verification/Partner Program) will also have the same outcome.

As we grow the time frame will grow but the year to reach will stay the same. You are able to apply for verification at 2k following if you have an active viewing of 5k a video.

As Gilleo grows the process of verification and earning commission from videos will be required to go through a set of qualifications to make sure everyone gets their chance. Below we have set some information that will be required, please keep in mind some more requirements may be added or change later-on to suit a growing need...

First Time Creators

Getting to be a known Creator on Gilleo is a little bit easy, just keep at it and follow the required details, you should be verified sooner than you think

● Must have over 5k followers (early apply at 2k)

● Must have been on Gilleo more than 3 months

● Must have over 150 viewing in for yourself (c-hrs 1500)

● Be of age 18yr to qualify, if under must have parents register for you

● Must have 5k of video viewing from other users (c-hr 50k)

● Must be at "Good" standing for more than 6 months

Known Creators

Creators that have a big presence in other communities will be required to do a shorter path than the ones that's just starting off.

● Must be over 50k following

● (For CZ) must be active on site for more than 3 months

● Must have their profile set up at 100% info completion
